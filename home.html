About

Let me introduce myself.

Previously worked in the Accounting and Compliance industries, I decided to make a career change and pursue my interest in technology as I have always been a tech savvy nerd.

I took the Web Development Immersive course at General Assembly and I enjoyed everything I learnt in the course. From GA, I learnt the fundamentals of web development and computer programming. It helped me to build a solid foundation for continuous learning of programming languages. I am determined to develop my skills in software/web development and grow professionally. I am confident in my ability to adapt and take on challenges.
